# ZephyrSync

**Seamless Media Orchestration for the Discerning Digital Archivist**

In an age where content flows like a restless river, ZephyrSync emerges as your personal media librarian—not merely a downloader, but a sophisticated bridge between the boundless expanse of online video and the organized tranquility of your local storage. Born from the same lineage that powers thousands of site extractions, ZephyrSync reimagines the process as a fine art: intuitive, respectful of your privacy, and utterly transparent. Whether you're preserving a lecture for offline study, compiling a curated playlist for a road trip, or archiving a channel's entire creative journey, ZephyrSync transforms a complex technical task into a delightful, one-click experience.

---

## 🌟 Overview

ZephyrSync is an open-source, cross-platform GUI application that elevates media downloading from a chore to a craft. It leverages the robust foundation of yt-dlp but wraps it in a user-friendly interface that feels both powerful and approachable. Think of it as your personal media concierge—always ready, never intrusive, and capable of handling the most demanding requests with grace.

Built for the creative professional, the curious student, and the media enthusiast alike, ZephyrSync offers a sanctuary from intrusive ads, convoluted command lines, and platform limitations. It respects your time and your bandwidth, delivering pristine results every time.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🎨 Elegant, Adaptive Interface
- **Responsive Design**: Flows seamlessly from a 4K monitor to a tablet or phone browser.
- **Dark & Light Modes**: Choose the ambiance that suits your workflow.
- **Multilingual Support**: Interface available in 10+ languages, breaking down barriers globally.

### 📡 Unparalleled Media Compatibility
- **2000+ Website Support**: From major platforms (YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion) to niche communities.
- **High-Fidelity Preservation**: Capture in 4K, 8K, HDR, 1080p60, or original source quality.
- **Playlist & Channel Archiving**: Download entire collections with a single action, preserving metadata and structure.
- **Subtitle & Chapter Extraction**: Immerse yourself with synchronized subtitles and built-in chapter markers.

### ⚙️ Intelligent Workflow
- **Smart Queue Management**: Prioritize, pause, resume, or reorder downloads without missing a beat.
- **Format Pre-Processing**: Automatically convert to MP4, MKV, WebM, or audio-only formats (MP3, FLAC, Opus).
- **Scheduled Downloads**: Set a timer for off-peak hours to save bandwidth.

### 🔒 Privacy-First Architecture
- **No User Accounts Required**: Your activity stays on your machine, period.
- **Zero Telemetry**: No data sent to external servers unless you explicitly choose to check for updates.
- **Open Source Transparency**: The code is yours to inspect, modify, and trust.

## 🔄 Workflow Example: The Curated Collection

1. **Launch**: Open ZephyrSync. The clean interface greets you without clutter.
2. **Paste**: Drop a playlist or channel URL into the input field.
3. **Configure**: Choose your desired quality (e.g., 1080p HDR with subtitles).
4. **Execute**: Click "Sync." The queue fills, and progress bars animate smoothly.
5. **Enjoy**: Your files land in a neatly organized folder, named and structured according to your preferences.

## 🧩 Advanced Usage

### Custom Output Templates
Define your own file naming conventions using variables: `%(title)s - %(uploader)s [%(resolution)s].%(ext)s`

### Post-Processing Scripts
Run custom commands after each download (e.g., compress for mobile, tag with metadata).

### Proxy & VPN Support
Route traffic through a proxy for regional content access or privacy enhancement.

### Batch Mode
Load a `.txt` file with hundreds of URLs, and let ZephyrSync process them overnight.
